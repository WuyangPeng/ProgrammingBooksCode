"For skills to hone, welcome home!"

Welcome to the supplemental CD. Efforts have been made to make this a very easy to use resource for you before, during, and after reading Imperfect C++.

On this CD you will find:

  • The entire suite of test/example programs in source form shown throughout the book listed in an organized form.
  • Articles relating to various chapters of the book, and to the libraries included on the CD.
  • The Arturius compiler multiplexer. (Well, sort of.)
  • C/C++ libraries mentioned in the book and/or the ones I recommended.
  • Errata and general frivulous items of business.
  • Free1 industry standard compilers used in the book, and links to others.
  • Tools of interest to developers 1.
  • Miscellaneous resources, such as links2 to journals programming languages documentation and books.
